# 📘 Module A — Excel Financial Modeling

## 📌 Objective
To build a structured financial model analyzing Grab Holdings’ multi-segment revenue performance, growth trends, and profitability.

---

## 🧾 Dataset
- Grab Holdings' publicly available investor reports
- Segments analyzed:
  - Deliveries
  - Mobility
  - Financial Services

---

## 🧠 Approach

### 1. Data Structuring
- Organized raw financial data into a clean tabular format
- Standardized revenue and cost metrics across segments
- Prepared data for time-series analysis (FY2023–FY2025)

### 2. Revenue Analysis
- Calculated total revenue by segment
- Computed Year-over-Year (YoY) growth rates
- Identified the fastest-growing segment

### 3. Profitability Analysis
- Derived Adjusted EBITDA per segment
- Calculated EBITDA margins
- Compared profitability across business units

### 4. Trend Evaluation
- Analyzed performance across multiple years
- Highlighted growth vs profitability trade-offs

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Total revenue showed strong upward growth across all segments
- **Deliveries** contributed the largest share of total revenue
- **Mobility** emerged as the most profitable segment (highest margins)
- **Financial Services** demonstrated the fastest growth but remained in an investment phase (lower profitability)

- Financial modeling in Excel
- Data cleaning and structuring
- YoY growth calculations
- Margin analysis (EBITDA)
- Comparative segment analysis
